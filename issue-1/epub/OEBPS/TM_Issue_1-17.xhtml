STAFF

Andy McMillan

Andy McMillan is a creator of things and the founder of Fiction. Through it he curates Build, an annual week-long event for web designers in Belfast, Northern Ireland and publishes The Manual, which you’re reading right now.

Carolyn Wood

Carolyn is a writer, author, editor, content strategist, and consultant through her company, Pixelingo, and has special interests in typography, design, and user experience. She is editor of Codex, the journal of typography and was acquisitions editor for three years at A List Apart.

Jez Burrows

Jez Burrows is a designer and illustrator based in Edinburgh, whose clients include Nike, the New York Times, WNYC, and Wired magazine. He is cofounder of Sing Statistics, an independent press publishing fiction and visual art.
