STAFF

Andy McMillan

Andy McMillan is a creator of things and the founder of Fiction, a made-up company responsible for Build, a week-long annual web design festival in Belfast, Northern Ireland; and The Manual, which you’re reading right now.

Carolyn Wood

Carolyn Wood is a writer, author, editor, content strategist, and founder of her company, Pixelingo. She has special interests in typography, design, and creating new enterprises with clients. She is former editor of Codex and was acquisitions editor for three years at A List Apart.

Jez Burrows

Jez Burrows is a designer and illustrator whose clients include O2, Nike, WNYC, the New York Times, WIRED magazine and others. He is cofounder of Sing Statistics, an independent press publishing fiction and visual art.
